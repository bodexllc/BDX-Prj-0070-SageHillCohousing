WELCOME

Welcome to the SageHill Cohousing (SHC) Neighborhood

SageHill Cohousing (SHC) is leading the move to a new generation of active adult neighborhoods.  A place where community is the centerpiece and shared resources and proximity make our home one of the most economical options available for aging-in-place.  A vibrant sustainable future – socially, physically, environmentally and economically.

SHC Communities are created and inspired by Senior Cohousing principles and processes. Together with future residents we are taking a proactive approach to building innovative “Pocket Neighborhoods” of 24-30 homes for us baby boomers to live out our lives among friends, with purpose, and in complete control.

Join us in planning your future SageHill Cohousing Community.
It is a journey of discovering, creating and thriving.  We are actively searching for three sites along Utah’s Wasatch Front and two in the Southern Utah region.  The first step is attending an “Orientation Event” where you will discover if a SHC community is the right investment for your future.

SHC Communities are created and inspired by Senior Cohousing principles and processes which are as much about self-examination and spiritual growth as they are about beautiful architecture, environmental ethics and intentional community.   ̃Cindy Turnquist, SageHill Cohousing Founder
